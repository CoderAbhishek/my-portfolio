The obvious question

You left 1Lattice in December 2025. What happened?

The project pipeline was thinning. Rather than wait for the next assignment in a holding pattern, I made a deliberate decision to step back, recalibrate, and invest the time in something I knew would compound — a focused upskilling period in AI, at a moment when the field is moving faster than most organisations can keep up with.

01 — The Decision

Why leave instead of waiting it out?

In consulting, there are moments between engagements where you can either coast or build. When the pipeline at 1Lattice thinned out, I assessed the situation clearly: the next assignment was uncertain in timing, and the opportunity cost of waiting was real.

AI capability is compounding every few months. The gap between professionals who are building with these tools and those who are merely aware of them is already significant — and widening. I chose to be on the right side of that gap, deliberately, with time and structure behind it.

That decision was active, not reactive. It was made with a plan, not out of frustration.
